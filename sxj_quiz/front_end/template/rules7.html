Round 7

Rules for Recall Your Memories


1. Every team will be shown a series of 7 words each.

2. Each word will be shown for 10 seconds.

3. After the series of words is finished, the team should recall the words in the order that is shown.

4. If a team answers the words in correct order, the team is awarded with 10 points. No points will be awarded if there is a break in the series of the words.

5. The questions in this round will not get passed.

6. The words displayed should not be jotted down by any means.
