Rules for Multiple Choice Round


1. Every team will be asked two MATHS questions with multiple choices.

2. Once the quiz master finishes reading the questions, the respondents will have 5seconds to answer the questions.

3. If answered correctly, they will be awarded 5 marks for the correct answer.

4. No points will be deducted for wrong questions

5. Questions will not get passed onto other teams in this round.
