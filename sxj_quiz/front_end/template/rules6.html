Round 6

Rules for Multiple Choice


1. Every team will be asked two questions with multiple choices.

2. Once the quiz master finishes reading the questions, the respondents will have 15 seconds to answer the questions.

3. If answered correctly, they will be awarded 10 marks for the correct answer.

4. If answered incorrectly, 5 points will be deducted.

5. Questions unanswered will also be considered incorrect.
