ROUND-5


Rules for Audio-Visual


1. Every team will be asked to identify a personality based on the audio or visual presented.

2. 10 points will be awarded for the correct answer.

3. Questions will not get passed on to the other teams.

4. The participants can respond to the questions only when the question is read completely.

Interruption of any kind in between will lead to the cancellation of the questions to the team.
